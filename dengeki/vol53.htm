Body: The Core Body
The core of all forms is the base body - TR-6 [Woundwort]. It is designed as a small MS with the assumption that its parts can be refitted, and is classified as a "light armor MS" with a frame that can be transformed and is minimally armored. It is equipped with folding mechanisms for its arms and legs to accommodate conversion to various forms. In addition, it is equipped with a high-power TR-type generator, giving it an excellent power-to-weight ratio. This is characteristic of a small MS. In addition, the torso section, which incorporates the cockpit, separates and transforms into a mobile pod--the Primrose II. It is also equipped with a high-power generator, allowing it to fly through the atmosphere in MA form.

：Conversion to an existing machine
This is a group of models that function as a replacement for existing Titans (Earth Federation Forces) aircraft by attaching parts of existing Titans (Earth Federation Forces) aircraft to the core of the body. This is a function in accordance with the "Model Integration Plan" to integrate the military's own weapon groups, which have lost compatibility due to the proliferation of prototypes, into a single TR-6 model, and by attaching parts of existing Titans (Earth Federation Forces) aircraft to its body, it functions as a replacement for all existing MS. This effectively made it possible to respond to the missions of existing machines. As a representative example, the Barzam II form is equipped with the arms and legs of the RX-154 Barzam. It serves as a replacement for the main MSs of the Earth Federation Forces, such as the Gundam and GM series. In its Hi-Zack II form, it is also equipped with RMS-106 Hi-Zack arms and legs. It serves as a replacement for the Principality of Zeon's military units such as the Hi-Zack and RMS-108 Marasai. The use of parts from existing machines in this way has the advantage of making it easier for pilots to switch from one model to another. The names of the machines are for convenience only, and there are an infinite number of possible combinations. The aircraft can be converted on the spot, or changed as needed depending on the situation.

TR-6: Equipped with support equipment
Each part of the TR-6 has a structure that can be converted into different operational methods by detaching and reassembling each part. The G-Parts can function alone as a support fighter, or when combined with an MS, as reinforced equipment. They can be attached to all existing MS in the military, and they are also general-purpose equipment with functions that enhance the capabilities of the unit to which they are attached. The basis of the Hrududu II is this Hrududu II form. The parts used in the core of the cockpit, torso, and subarms are common to the body. By attaching various special equipment to it, it can be transformed into a support machine for local warfare. In the Hambrabi II form, which uses Haze'n-thley II thrusters on both wings, and in the Aqua Hambrabi II form, in which it is converted to underwater equipment, the combined MS is given high underwater combat capability.

：Successor machine with exclusive equipment
Apart from the conversion to a replacement machine by installing existing parts, a form that is particularly important for the Titans' main objective of defending the Earth's sphere is the conversion to a successor machine with dedicated equipment. This refers to a group of models that incorporate expanded functions to overcome the high-performance machines of the assumed enemy force. In order to achieve this, dedicated equipment is prepared, and it assumes the role of successor to important local warfare MS. To cite one example, the Haze'n-thley II form is the successor to the RX-110 Gabthley, a high-speed combat MS with a combined separation mechanism and excellent operational flexibility. This machine has the ability to compete with the successors of the "Z Project" series promoted by the enemy ("AEUG") camp—the MSZ-010 ZZ Gundam, MSA-0011[Ext] Ex-S Gundam, etc. — It is a Gundam-type MS that serves as the flagship of the Titans. In its Kehaar II form, the Kehaar II is equipped with flight-enhancing parts on its bare body, and in addition to its anti-air defense duties on behalf of the NRX-044 Asshimar, it also serves as an escort aircraft for the Inle. In addition to this form, the Hrairoo II form is also included in this group. The main difference between this form and the "B: Conversion to existing aircraft" form, which is composed of existing parts, is that this form is composed of high-performance battle-use parts.

Super-heavy equipment form
Each form of the body is combined with the G-part "Hrududu II". Each of these forms is called the "Rah" form. In addition to raising the combined MS's capabilities to the forefront, the Rah form enables the attachment and operation of large MA-sized equipment called "super-heavy equipment" via the drum frames on both sides. With these functions, the TR-6 evolved into a giant MS form exceeding 40 meters, then into a giant MA form, and finally into a strategic weapon, expanding its functions. The starting point for this evolution is this group of forms. Like Woundwort-Rah II, which is a combination of Hrududu II and its base body, it can be combined with any MS. Furthermore, the form in which Haze'n-thley II and Hrududu II are combined is classified as an "area dominance machine" due to its advanced performance. In addition to the TR-6, it can also combine with existing machines such as the Hi-Zack and Barzam.

Giant conversion
A typical MS has a head height of around 20 meters. Corresponding to these height limitations are the "B: Conversion to existing machines" and "Successor machines with exclusive equipment" groups. On the other hand, however, many NT-specific MS, which are called "Giant Gods," have a top height of over 40 meters. The size of the machine allows it to carry special armaments that can be remotely controlled by the psycommu, high-powered firearms throughout its body, and extended equipment. By refitting its equipment, the TR-6 has the ability to expand not only its size but also its performance without limit. Among the equipment for conversion, a group of forms to compete with NT-only MS is also available. These include the "Gigantic Form," in which the limbs of MRX-009 Psycho Gundam are attached via Hrududu II, and the "Queenly Form," which is equipped with numerous weapon containers for its primary mission of base defense. In particular, in the "Queenly" form, the Kehaar II is used as a multifunctional binder, and the high applicability of the TR-6's reconfiguration mechanism can be seen here as well. In addition, there is also the [Full Armor Queenly] form, which is a form to counter the NZ-000 Queen Mansa, which was assumed to be a future threat. In this way, as with the other forms, the parts configuration of the giant MS forms can be flexibly changed, and evolution to other forms that can respond to and eliminate further threats can be envisioned. This expandability is the greatest advantage of the TR-6.

Final Form
In contrast to MSs, which are tactical-level local weapons, MAs, which are outside the humanoid format and are not limited by size, have the power to overturn the war situation with a single unit. It was the embodiment of the most powerful weapon sought by the Titans: the Gundam. The "TR Project" was a project that envisioned the completion of the "Inle" final weapon form, its mother ship, and its home port, the space base SSD, as well as the construction of an organizational structure for mass production and operation of the Inle. All of this is supported by a mechanism called the Universal Conversion System. Using the system's unlimited change and versatility as a weapon, the TR Project was looking toward a grand future. This Fiver II form is a ballistic weapon equipped with a composite binder that combines offense and defense. It was given the ability to single-handedly assault and suppress enemy bases. In addition, when combined with the Dandelion II, an MA form that takes charge of stratospheric defense against foreign enemies flying from outer space and threatening the peace of the earth sphere, it can function as the upper body of the Inle.
