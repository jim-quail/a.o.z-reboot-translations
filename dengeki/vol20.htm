Gundam TR-S [Hazel Hrair]. It is a Gundam-type MS that is the completed form of the “TR Project” promoted by the Titans. In this article, we will discuss the RX-123 Gundam TR-S [Rabscuttle] form, which is the earth's gatekeeper to prevent enemies from outer space.

Like the RX-107 Rosette, which is the core unit of the RX-107 TR-4 [Dandelion], the RX-123 Gundam TR-S [Hazel Hrair], which is the core unit, is installed inside the nose, and the binder covers the bare body, with reinforced parts.

From the time of departure to the destination, flight in MA form is the primary means of transportation.

A binder for stratospheric maneuvering which was tested and developed in TR-4 [Dandelion]. The completed form of this binder was the RX-124 Gundam TR-S [Hazel Hrair] equipped with the [Rabscuttle]. It was built for the purpose of waiting in the stratosphere to intercept invasions and attacks by enemy forces from outer space, being based on SSD deployed in Earth orbit. It has the same characteristics as the MAs of the former Principality of Zeon armed forces (especially the MA-05 Bigro series), and excels at hit-and-run tactics that take advantage of its mobility and claw arm. It can also fly through the stratosphere using its high mobility and can be deployed to any location on earth in a short time. The binders that cover its body also function as a lifting body, and like the Wave Rider, it can enter the atmosphere depending on the mission. After reaching the area of operation in MA form, it can transform into MF (Mobile Fortress) form for interception and stay there to defend the base by Minovsky craft. In order to cope with the G forces applied during maneuvering, it is recommended that they be operated by Cyber Newtypes.

The large claw arm has four fingers and a beam cannon in the center. By converging the beams, it can also be used as a beam saber. The internal frame is the same as the arms of the RX-124 Gundam TR-6 [Dandelion II], to which it has been fitted with additional external parts.

A device that blows air to cool the airframe. Used in conjunction with heat-resistant binders, it protects the airframe from the friction of atmospheric entry.

In the atmosphere, the shape is designed to take advantage of aerodynamic characteristics, including the generation of lift. When maneuvering in the stratosphere, it uses the reaction of the atmosphere to change its trajectory. During orbit change, the fuselage is rotated 180 degrees upside down to a dorsal flight state.

It is equipped with a TR-type generator. It uses a system that switches between jet propulsion in the atmosphere and rocket propulsion outside the atmosphere. The binder and generator hatch of the Dandelion II are visible through the air intake opening.

In the center of the fuselage is a diffuse beam cannon of the same type as the MRX-009 Psycho Gundam, and in its MA form, the right and left binders serve as particle convergence and acceleration plates, firing a beam with an extremely high convergence rate.

The Gundam TR-S [Hazel Hrair] is the crystallization of the technology established in the TR-1 to TR-5 series. The [Rabscuttle] is based on the experimental high-speed MA form that was tested in TR-4 [Dandelion] - an experimental machine equipped with a maneuverable binder, a large claw arm, and a weapon cargo.

[Dandelion II] was designed after the ‘Model Integration Project’ in the form of a successor to the ‘Rabscuttle’ concept. As a result, many common parts can be seen in each part.

The inside of the binder on the back side of the fuselage also functions as a weapon cargo bay, and armaments can be mounted according to the mission. It is also equipped with the El-Ahrairah, which is operated in tandem with the Rabscuttle, and its armament container for conversion, as well as anti-space missile weapons to intercept invading enemies, which is the key to its mission. In addition, the Daedalus Unit, a spherical Minovsky craft device, is mounted in the rear. It moves to the airspace of operation with these built-in, and deploys the mounted equipment after the transformation. While moving, the I-field equipped on the “El-Ahrairah” provides protection.
